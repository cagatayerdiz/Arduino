7 Segment Display Description
=============================

There are three 7 segment display in this project.

***7 Segment Display***

![alt tag](http://i60.tinypic.com/4he369.gif)

0 - a, b, c, d, e, f

1 - b, c 

2 - a, b, d, e, g

3 - a, b, c, d, g

4 - b, c, f, g

5 - a, c, d, f, g

6 - a, c, d, e, f, g

7 - a, b, c

8 - a, b, c, d, e, f, g

9 - a, b, c, d, f, g

10 - a, b, c, e, f, g

11 - c, d, e, f, g

12 - a, d, e, f

13 - b, c, d, e, g

14 - a, d, e, f, g

15 - a, e, f, g

***7 Segment Display 3 Digit***

There are extra 3 inputs whic are digit1, digit2 and digit3

![alt tag](http://i58.tinypic.com/15qzo1e.png)

dig1 - 3.3 v

a - pin 13

b - pin 12

c - pin 11

d - pin 10

e - pin 9

f - pin 8

g - pin 7
